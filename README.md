# 💡 KeenKeeper

## 📝 Description

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management website designed to help you nurture your friendships. It allows you to track your interactions, set connection goals, monitor the status of your relationships ("On Track" or "Need Attention"), and view your communication habits over time to ensure you stay close with the people who matter most.

## 💻 Key Features

1. **Friendship Dashboard & Status Tracking:** Monitor all your connections at a glance. See total friends, quickly identify who is "On Track" or "Needs Attention," and track who you plan to connect with this month.
2. **Interaction Timeline:** Log and review a detailed history of your interactions—whether it's a meetup, phone call, text, or video chat—so you always know the last time you spoke.
3. **Friendship Analytics & Profiles:** Dive deep into individual friend profiles to see relationship goals and use the analytics dashboard to visualize your communication habits by interaction type.
